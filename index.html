  • About the project

    This project is part of my doctoral thesis, named "Evolution of Opinions: Opinion Diversity, Dynamic and Social Control." In this research, I'm aimed to develop an ideal point estimator which can precisely locate users in a latent high-dimensional ideological space from what they have posted online and whom they have interacted with.

    About me

    My name is Zhu Yuner. I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate at Journalism & Media Studies Centre, HKU. I'm interested in investigating the intertwined relationship between social media and social system.
